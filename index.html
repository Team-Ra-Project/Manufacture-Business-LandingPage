The Reality

The digital challenges manufacturers actually face

Buyers, distributors and exporters research a supplier long before they ever pick up the phone. If your business is hard to find, looks less established than it should, or takes too long to respond, the enquiry goes to whoever answered first — not necessarily whoever makes the better product.

This shows up in ways that rarely get tracked on a balance sheet, but quietly shape how much business a manufacturer wins.
